What We Check

Token Distribution

We examine how tokens are distributed between the owner and the community, checking for fairness, transparency, vesting schedules, and mechanisms to prevent unfair concentration.

Owner Privileges

Evaluation of owner powers like minting, fund access, parameter changes, and upgrade rights, with focus on safeguards against misuse.

Governance

Assessing voting power distribution, DAO structures, and transparency in decision-making to ensure fair participation.

Vesting and Locks

Analysis of token locking and vesting mechanisms to prevent sudden dumps and protect market stability.

Hidden Risks & Transparency

Economic Security & Controls

Integration Safety

Why Ethereum?

Ethereum stands out as the premier blockchain network for smart contracts due to its unparalleled transparency and security. Unlike other networks such as Solana, Ethereum ensures that all smart contracts are fully visible and auditable by anyone. This transparency fosters trust among developers and investors alike, as every aspect of a contract's code is accessible for review and verification.

Full Transparency

Every smart contract on Ethereum is open-source, allowing for complete visibility and auditability. This openness reduces the risk of hidden vulnerabilities and promotes a secure environment for decentralized applications.

Enhanced Security

Ethereum's robust security protocols ensure that smart contracts are resistant to exploits and attacks. The extensive developer community continuously audits and improves the network's security measures.

Widespread Adoption

As the first smart contract platform, Ethereum boasts a vast ecosystem of developers, projects, and users. This widespread adoption ensures continuous innovation and support for the network.
